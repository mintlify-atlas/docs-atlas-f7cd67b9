## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Fetch a Reddit thread">
    The bot connects to Reddit via the PRAW API and pulls a post from your chosen subreddit — either a specific post or one picked automatically from the hot feed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate voiceovers">
    Each comment (or the post body in story mode) is converted to speech using your chosen TTS engine. Supports TikTok, ElevenLabs, OpenAI, AWS Polly, Google Translate TTS, Streamlabs Polly, and pyttsx3.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Capture Reddit screenshots">
    Playwright takes screenshots of the Reddit post and comments using the Reddit web UI, respecting your light/dark/transparent theme setting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Compose the final video">
    FFmpeg composites the comment screenshots and voiceovers over a looping gameplay background video (Minecraft, GTA, Rocket League, and more), with optional background music.
  </Step>
</Steps>
